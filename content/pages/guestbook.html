Guestbook

We used to put guestbooks on websites. They were a way for visitors to drop a note to webmasters saying "I made it, I'm here!" The internet used to be a way to connect a person to another person—now it connects people to the masses.

I want to bring us back to the mentality we had before. I want you, the visitor, to tell me you made it. I don't want to connect to an audience, I want to, through the power of the Internet, connect myself to you.

So I made a guestbook, and I'd really like you to sign it. Leave your name. Your email, if you want. The URL of your website, if you have one. And leave a note. Please be nice—remember, you're dropping a note to another human.
